Batch Image Conversion: How to Optimize Hundreds of Images Instantly

Time is money, especially for e-commerce owners. Instead of editing one photo at a time, batch image conversion allows you to process entire folders in seconds.

What is Batch Conversion?

Batch image conversion is the practice of applying the same operations—resizing, compressing, or format changing—to a large group of images simultaneously. It ensures consistency across your entire website.

Why It’s Essential for E-commerce

  • Consistency: Uniform dimensions for all your product images.
  • Performance: Quick compression helps you pass Google’s Core Web Vitals.
  • Automation: Free up hours of your work week for marketing and growth.
