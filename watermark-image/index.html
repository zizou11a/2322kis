Why Add a Watermark to Your Images?

A watermark is the simplest way to protect your creative work online. Whether you're a photographer, designer, blogger, or content creator, watermarking images before sharing them establishes ownership and deters unauthorized use — without requiring legal action.

Watermarks are also used professionally to brand product photos for e-commerce, add copyright notices to stock photography, and mark draft or proof images sent to clients. A semi-transparent watermark preserves image visibility while making your claim of ownership clear.
