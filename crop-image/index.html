Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. IMGVO processes all images entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.

What aspect ratio should I use for Instagram?

Use 1:1 (square) for feed posts, 4:5 for portrait posts, and 9:16 for Stories and Reels.

What is the best format to save a cropped image?

Use PNG if you need transparency or sharp edges (logos, screenshots). Use JPG for photos. Use WebP for web use — it offers the best balance of quality and file size.

Can I crop PNG images with transparency?

Yes. IMGVO preserves the alpha channel when cropping PNG files. The transparent areas remain transparent in the output.
