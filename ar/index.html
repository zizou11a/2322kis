Yes, ImgSwift is completely free with no signup required. All image conversion and compression features are available at no cost, with no file limits.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. ImgSwift processes all images entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the Canvas API. Your files never leave your device and are never sent to any server — your privacy is fully protected.

ImgSwift supports 8 output formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, ICO, and PDF. You can convert from any common image format (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and more) to any of these formats.

How much can I compress an image?

You can reduce image file size by up to 90% using the compression quality slider. You have full control over the quality level — from maximum compression (smallest file) to minimum compression (best quality).

Can I convert or compress multiple images at once?

Yes. ImgSwift supports batch processing. Simply select or drag multiple images at once. All files are converted or compressed in parallel and downloaded automatically.
