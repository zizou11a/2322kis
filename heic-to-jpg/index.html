Frequently Asked Questions

What is a HEIC file?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format used by iPhones since iOS 11. It offers better quality at smaller file sizes than JPG, but many apps and websites don't support it yet.

Why can't I open HEIC files on Windows or Android?

HEIC is an Apple format and is not natively supported on Windows or most Android devices. Converting to JPG makes your photos universally compatible with all devices, apps, and websites.
